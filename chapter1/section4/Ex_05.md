### 1.4.5 

Give tilde approximations for the following quantities:

​	a. $N+1$

​	b. $1+1/N$

​	c. $(1+1/N)(1+2/N)$

​	d. $2N^3-15N^2+N$

​	e. $lg(2N)/lgN$

​	f. $lg(N^2+1) / lgN$

​	g. $N^{100}/2^N$



**Answer:**

​	a. $N+1$											$N$

​	b. $1+1/N$										$1$

​	c. $(1+1/N)(1+2/N)$	  			$1$

​	d. $2N^3-15N^2+N$		  			$N^3$

​	e. $lg(2N)/lgN$					  			$1$

​	f. $lg(N^2+1) / lgN$				       	$2$

​	g. $N^{100}/2^N$										$0$
