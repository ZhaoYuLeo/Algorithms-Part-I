### 3.3.22
Find a sequence of keys to insert into a BST and into a red-black BST such that the 
height of the BST is less than the height of the red-black BST, or prove that no such
sequence is possible.

**Answer**:

BST:

4 2 6 1 5 3

```
    4
  2   6
1  3 5
```

red-black BST:

```
        4
      3*  6
     2  5
   1*
```
